## Keeping Our Community Safe

Your reports help us maintain a positive gaming environment. We take all reports seriously and act quickly to address violations of our community guidelines.

## How to Report

### Quick Report Options

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Report a Message">
    **In-Chat Reporting:**
    1. Click the three dots (⋮) on any message
    2. Select "Report Message"
    3. Choose violation type
    4. Add context (optional but helpful)
    5. Submit report
    
    <Tip>
      Screenshots are automatically captured with message reports
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Report a User">
    **Profile Reporting:**
    1. Go to user's profile
    2. Click "Report User" button
    3. Select primary concern
    4. Provide specific examples
    5. Attach evidence if available
    6. Submit for review
    
    Be specific about what happened
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Report Content">
    **Other Content:**
    - Gaming clips/images
    - Profile content
    - Group posts
    - Event descriptions
    
    Use the flag icon on any content
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  Knowingly filing false reports is a violation of our guidelines and may result in account action.
</Warning>

## What to Include in Reports

### Effective Reporting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Be Specific">
    Instead of: "User was mean"
    Try: "User called me [specific slurs] after losing match"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide Context">
    - When did it happen?
    - What led to the incident?
    - Was it a one-time or repeated behavior?
    - Which game/mode were you playing?
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Include Evidence">
    - Screenshots of chat
    - Video clips
    - Voice recording (if available)
    - Match IDs
    - Timestamps
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Stay Factual">
    - Report what happened
    - Avoid assumptions about intent
    - Don't exaggerate
    - Be honest about your role
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The more detail you provide, the faster and more accurately we can investigate
</Info>

## Our Moderation Process

### How Reports Are Handled

<Card title="Investigation Timeline" icon="clock">
  **Step 1: Initial Review (0-2 hours)**
  - AI scans for immediate threats
  - Urgent issues escalated
  - Automatic actions for clear violations
  
  **Step 2: Human Review (2-24 hours)**
  - Moderator investigates
  - Context examined
  - Evidence verified
  - History checked
  
  **Step 3: Action Decision (24-48 hours)**
  - Appropriate action determined
  - User notified
  - Report resolved
  - Follow-up if needed
</Card>

### Moderation Actions

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Warnings">
    **First-Time/Minor Violations:**
    - Educational warning
    - Reminder of guidelines
    - No restrictions
    - Noted on record
    
    Users can receive 2 warnings
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Restrictions">
    **Repeated/Moderate Violations:**
    - Chat restrictions (1-7 days)
    - Matchmaking limits
    - Feature restrictions
    - Public profile hiding
    
    Length depends on severity
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Suspensions">
    **Serious Violations:**
    - Account suspended (7-30 days)
    - All features blocked
    - Connections notified
    - Appeal available
    
    Requires significant violation
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Bans">
    **Severe/Repeated Violations:**
    - Permanent ban
    - Device/IP included
    - No new accounts
    - Limited appeal options
    
    Reserved for worst offenses
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## After You Report

### What Happens Next

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    You'll receive:
    - Report ID number
    - Confirmation email
    - Estimated timeline
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Investigation">
    We will:
    - Review all evidence
    - Check user history
    - Verify claims
    - Make fair decision
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Resolution">
    You'll be notified:
    - Action was taken (no specifics)
    - Case closed
    - Any follow-up needed
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  We don't share specific punishments to protect user privacy and prevent retaliation
</Note>

### Checking Report Status

View your reports:
1. Go to Settings → Safety
2. Click "My Reports"
3. See status of each report
4. View resolution notices

## Emergency Situations

### Immediate Action Needed

<Warning>
  For real-world emergencies, contact local law enforcement immediately - don't wait for platform response
</Warning>

**Urgent Reports:**
- Email: urgent@matchandplay.com
- Subject: URGENT - [Brief description]
- Include all relevant information
- We respond within 2 hours

**Situations Requiring Urgent Report:**
- Real-world threats
- Self-harm mentions
- Child safety concerns
- Planned harassment campaigns
- Doxxing attempts

## Appeals Process

### If You're Reported

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Receive Action" icon="envelope">
    You'll get:
    - Email notification
    - Reason for action
    - Duration (if temporary)
    - Appeal instructions
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Submit Appeal" icon="file-lines">
    Appeals must include:
    - Ticket number
    - Your explanation
    - Any evidence
    - Why action was wrong
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Appeal Guidelines

**Do:**
- Be respectful and professional
- Provide new information
- Admit mistakes if applicable
- Explain misunderstandings
- Show you understand guidelines

**Don't:**
- Submit multiple appeals
- Threaten or insult staff
- Deny clear evidence
- Create new accounts
- Have others appeal for you

<Info>
  Appeals are reviewed by senior moderators not involved in original decision
</Info>

## False Reporting

### Consequences of Abuse

False reports waste resources and harm innocent users:

- First offense: Warning
- Second offense: Reporting restricted
- Third offense: Account action

**What Constitutes False Reporting:**
- Knowingly false claims
- Coordinated mass reporting
- Revenge reporting
- Report spam
- Manipulating evidence
