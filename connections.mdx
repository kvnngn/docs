## Sending Connection Requests

### Making a Great First Impression

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a Player">
    Through discovery, game lobbies, or friend suggestions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Their Profile">
    Check compatibility:
    - Shared games
    - Matching schedules
    - Similar playstyles
    - Communication preferences
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send Personalized Request">
    Include a message that:
    - Mentions a shared game
    - References their profile
    - Suggests when to play
    - Shows genuine interest
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Personalized messages have 3x higher acceptance rates than generic requests!
</Tip>

### Request Templates

Speed up connections with smart templates:

<Tabs>

  <Tab title="Casual">

  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Learning">

  </Tab>

## Connection Maintenance

### Keeping Relationships Active

<Warning>
  Connections may be archived after 6 months of inactivity. Reactivate anytime!
</Warning>

**Stay Connected:**
- Send occasional messages
- React to their updates
- Invite to new games
- Share gaming news
- Schedule regular sessions

### Pruning Your List

**When to Remove Connections:**
- No longer play same games
- Incompatible schedules now
- Negative gaming experiences
- Account inactive for 1+ year
- Personal preference changes
