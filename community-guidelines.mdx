## Building a Better Gaming Community

Match & Play is committed to fostering a welcoming, inclusive environment where all gamers can connect, play, and have fun together. These guidelines help ensure everyone has a positive experience.

<Card title="Our Core Values" icon="heart">
  - **Respect** - Treat all gamers with dignity
  - **Inclusion** - Welcome players of all backgrounds
  - **Fair Play** - Compete with integrity
  - **Safety** - Protect our community members
  - **Fun** - Remember why we game!
</Card>

## Expected Behavior

### Be a Great Community Member

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Respect Others">
    **Show Respect**
    - Accept different skill levels
    - Respect gaming preferences
    - Honor communication choices
    - Value diverse perspectives
    
    **Inclusive Gaming**
    - Welcome new players
    - Be patient with beginners
    - Offer help when asked
    - Celebrate others' achievements
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Communicate Positively">
    **Good Communication**
    - Use friendly language
    - Give constructive feedback
    - Stay calm during heated moments
    - Apologize for mistakes
    
    **Gaming Etiquette**
    - GG (Good Game) after matches
    - No rage quitting
    - Communicate team strategies
    - Respect in-game roles
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Play Fair">
    **Fair Competition**
    - No cheating or exploits
    - Play to win, but fairly
    - Accept losses gracefully
    - Report bugs, don't abuse them
    
    **Honest Profiles**
    - Accurate skill ratings
    - Real availability times
    - Truthful game preferences
    - Genuine intentions
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Age-Appropriate Gaming

### Protecting Young Gamers

Match & Play is for players 13 and older. We take extra steps to protect younger members:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Age Verification">
    - Required birthdate on signup
    - Age-appropriate matching
    - Restricted features for minors
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Minor Protections">
    - No adult content exposure
    - Limited personal info sharing
    - Parental controls available
    - Extra moderation for young users
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adult Responsibilities">
    - Be mindful of younger players
    - Keep content age-appropriate
    - Model good behavior
    - Report concerning interactions
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Parents: Visit our [Parental Controls](/safety/parental-controls) page to manage your child's experience
</Info>

## Reporting System

### How to Report Issues

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="In-Message Reporting">
    **Quick Report**
    1. Click the three dots on any message
    2. Select "Report Message"
    3. Choose the violation type
    4. Add details (optional)
    5. Submit report
    
    Reports are anonymous to the reported user
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Profile Reporting">
    **Report a User**
    1. Visit their profile
    2. Click "Report User"
    3. Select violation category
    4. Provide evidence/context
    5. Submit for review
    
    Include screenshots when possible
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Emergency Reporting">
    **Immediate Threats**
    - Email: safety@matchandplay.com
    - Include all relevant information
    - Mark as "URGENT" in subject
    - We respond within 2 hours
    
    For real-world threats, contact local authorities first
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### What Happens After Reporting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Immediate Review">
    AI systems check for obvious violations and take instant action if needed
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Human Review">
    Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Investigation">
    We may request additional information or review chat history
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Action Taken">
    Appropriate consequences applied based on severity and history
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Follow-up">
    You'll receive confirmation that action was taken (specifics remain confidential)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enforcement Actions

### Consequence System

Our moderation team uses a progressive discipline system:

<Card title="Violation Consequences" icon="gavel">
  **Minor Violations**
  - Warning message
  - Temporary chat restriction
  - Feature limitations
  
  **Moderate Violations**
  - 24-72 hour suspension
  - Matchmaking restrictions
  - Public profile hiding
  
  **Severe Violations**
  - 7-30 day suspension
  - Permanent feature bans
  - Account termination
</Card>

### Appeals Process

Believe a decision was made in error?

1. **Submit Appeal**: Through your account settings
2. **Provide Context**: Explain your side clearly
3. **Wait for Review**: Appeals reviewed within 72 hours
4. **Final Decision**: Appeals team decision is final

<Note>
  Only one appeal per action is allowed. Be thorough in your explanation.
</Note>
