## Your Safety Matters

Match & Play is committed to creating a safe gaming environment for everyone. Our comprehensive safety features give you control over your experience while protecting you from harmful behavior.

## Privacy Controls

### Profile Privacy Settings

<Tabs>
  <Tab title='Public Profile'>
    **Visible to Everyone:** - Username and avatar - Bio (optional) - General
    game library - Basic availability (morning/evening/night) - General location
    (country/region) **Control Options:** - Hide specific games - Limit bio
    visibility - Hide location entirely - Private profile mode
  </Tab>

  <Tab title='Connected Users'>
    **Visible to Connections:** - Full gaming schedule - Platform gamertags -
    Detailed game stats - Voice chat preferences - Session history together
    **Customization:** - Per-connection privacy - Hide specific platforms -
    Stats visibility toggle - Schedule granularity
  </Tab>

  <Tab title='Private Information'>
    **Never Shared:** - Email address - Real name (unless you add it) - Payment
    information - IP address - Exact location - Device information
    <Warning>
      Never share personal information like phone numbers or addresses in
      messages
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Visibility Controls

<Card title='Who Can Find You' icon='eye'>
  Configure discovery settings: - **Everyone**: Maximum visibility for finding
  players - **Friends of Friends**: Extended network only - **Connections
  Only**: Just your accepted connections - **Nobody**: Complete privacy mode You
  can also: - Block specific regions/countries - Hide from search temporarily -
  Appear offline while browsing - Use anonymous mode
</Card>

## Best Practices

### Stay Safe Online

<Check>Follow these guidelines for the best experience:</Check>

1. **Protect Personal Info**: Never share real name, address, phone
2. **Meet Safely**: If meeting IRL, choose public places
3. **Trust Your Instincts**: Block anyone making you uncomfortable
4. **Report Issues**: Help keep the community safe
5. **Use Platform Features**: Keep communication on Match & Play

### Red Flags to Watch

<Warning>
  Be cautious of users who: - Ask for personal information - Want to move chat
  off-platform quickly - Request money or gifts - Make you uncomfortable - Seem
  too good to be true - Pressure you to share more
</Warning>
