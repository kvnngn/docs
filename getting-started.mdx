## Welcome to Your Gaming Community

Getting started with Match & Play is quick and easy. In just a few minutes, you'll be discovering gamers who share your passion and schedule.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title='Sign Up'>
    Visit [Match & Play](https://matchandplay.io) and click **Sign Up**. You can
    create an account using: - Google (recommended for quick setup) - Email
    address - Discord account (coming soon)
  </Step>

  <Step title='Verify Your Email'>
    If you signed up with email, check your inbox for a verification link. Click
    it to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title='Choose Your Username'>
    Pick a unique gaming username that represents you. This is how other players
    will find and recognize you.
    <Note>
      Choose wisely! Usernames can only be changed once every 30 days.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Build Your Gaming Passport

Your Gaming Passport is your complete gaming identity. It helps us match you with compatible players.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title='Gaming Platforms'>
    Select all platforms you game on: - PC (Steam, Epic Games, Battle.net) -
    PlayStation (PS4/PS5) - Xbox (Xbox One/Series X|S) - Nintendo Switch -
    Mobile (iOS/Android)
    <Tip>
      Add your gamertags for each platform to make it easy for new friends to
      add you!
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title='Favorite Games'>
    Add your top games: 1. Search for games by title 2. Select your skill level
    (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert) 3. Choose your preferred play
    styles (Competitive, Casual, Both) 4. Add any notes about what you're
    looking for The more games you add, the better your matches will be!
  </Tab>

  <Tab title='Gaming Schedule'>
    Set your typical gaming availability: - Choose your timezone - Mark your
    usual gaming hours for each day - Toggle "Available Now" when you're ready
    to play
    <Info>
      Your availability helps us show you players who are online when you are.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 3: Customize Your Preferences

Fine-tune your experience to find your perfect gaming companions:

### Communication Style

- **Voice Chat Preferred**: For coordinated gameplay
- **Text Only**: If you prefer typing
- **Flexible**: Open to either option

### Matching Preferences

- **Age Range**: Find players in your age group
- **Language**: Select languages you're comfortable gaming in
- **Region**: Prioritize players with low ping

### Gaming Goals

- **Competitive Ranking**: Climb the ladder together
- **Casual Fun**: Just enjoy the game
- **Learning & Improvement**: Find mentors or students
- **Social & Community**: Make lasting friendships

## Step 5: Make Your First Connection

Found someone interesting? Here's how to connect:

1. **View Their Profile**: Check their Gaming Passport for compatibility
2. **Send a Friend Request**: Include a personalized message
3. **Start Chatting**: Once accepted, you can message directly
4. **Game Together**: Coordinate your next gaming session!

<Check>
  Pro tip: Mention a specific game or interest from their profile in your first
  message!
</Check>
