## Your Personal Game Collection

Your Game Library is the heart of your Gaming Passport, powered by the comprehensive IGDB database. It helps you organize your games, track your progress, and find players for every title you love.

## Tips & Best Practices

### Optimize Your Library

<Check>
  Follow these tips for best results:
</Check>

1. **Quality over Quantity**: Focus on games you actually play
2. **Stay Current**: Update skills as you improve
3. **Be Specific**: Add notes about what you want
4. **Pin Wisely**: Feature your most-played games
5. **Regular Updates**: Archive games you've stopped playing

### Common Mistakes

<Warning>
  Avoid these library pitfalls:
  - Adding every game you've ever touched
  - Never updating skill levels
  - No notes or preferences
  - Hiding too many games
  - Forgetting platform specifics
</Warning>
