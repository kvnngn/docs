## Trend Analysis

### Why Games Trend

<Warning>
  Trending doesn't always mean good - research before buying!
</Warning>

**Common Trend Triggers:**
1. **Major Update**: New content drops
2. **Streamer Effect**: Big names playing
3. **Esports Event**: Tournament hype
4. **Sale/Free Period**: Accessibility boost
5. **Viral Moment**: Meme or clip explosion
6. **Season Launch**: Fresh competitive start

### Reading the Trends

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Duration">

    - Flash trend (1-2 days)
