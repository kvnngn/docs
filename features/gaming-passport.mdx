## Your Complete Gaming Identity

Your Gaming Passport is the cornerstone of Match & Play - a dynamic profile that showcases who you are as a gamer and helps you find perfect gaming companions.

<Card title="What is a Gaming Passport?" icon="passport">
  A Gaming Passport is your all-in-one gaming profile that includes:
  - Your gaming platforms and IDs
  - Favorite games and skill levels
  - Availability schedule
  - Play style preferences
  - Gaming achievements
  - Social connections
</Card>
